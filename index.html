Frequently Asked Questions

What is IoT?

IoT stands for Internet of Things, referring to the interconnection of everyday devices to the internet.

How i works?

IoT devices can communicate with each other, a process called machine-to-machine (M2M) communication. Humans can interact with the devices to set them up, give them instructions, or access the data

How can I use these products?

You can integrate them into your daily life to enhance convenience and efficiency.

What is the market growth in IoT?

The IoT market is expected to grow at a rate of 26.4% between 2022 and 2029. The number of connected devices in the world is projected to surpass 25.4 billion by 2030

Some examples of IoT?

You likely use IoT devices every day. Some that you may be familiar with include smart thermostats and kitchen appliances, fitness-tracking watches, self-driving cars, and home security systems. Personal medical devices like pacemakers are also IoT devices.

A fun fact About IoT

The first IoT device was a soda machine at Carnegie Mellon, which led to the development of the first widely used IoT device—ATMs.
